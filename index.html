Welcome to neji!

neji is a site for learning and practicing Japanese. When learning a language it is often diffiult to find situations to practice what we learn.

Being able to communicate means having the ability to say what you want, quickly and effortlessly. With that in mind neji fouccusses on repition of key skills to the point that they become second nature.

Then when you are having a converation you can spend you time worrying about what you want to say and not how to say it!
